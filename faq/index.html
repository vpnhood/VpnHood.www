General

VpnHood is a free VPN powered by the VpnHood ENGINE, fully developed by the VpnHood team. It uses proprietary protocols built on open-source foundations for maximum transparency. Our open-source code is available for public review to verify its security and integrity. VpnHood is designed to optimize data transmission for high-performance connectivity and enhanced network compatibility. We prioritize your privacy by ensuring zero collection of personal information and activity logs, providing a highly secure and private digital experience.

VpnHood! CONNECT is a free VPN that users can enjoy without limitations. However, while VpnHood! CLIENT is also free, it does not include servers for direct connections. Instead, those who set up their own dedicated VPN using VpnHood! MANAGER can share this app with their users along with a Server Key for access.

Both VpnHood! CONNECT and VpnHood! CLIENT are compatible with Android, Android TV, and Windows. However, VpnHood! CONNECT on Windows is not free; you'll need to purchase a Premium Key, which is available on the pricing page.
