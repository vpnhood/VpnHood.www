## Sign up for VpnHood Manager

**Email address.** We store your email address and public profile as part of the registration process. It is necessary to create a VpnHood Manager account.  

## VpnHood Server IP

The VpnHood Server IPs, versions, and countries are collected and saved in the VpnHood Manager database. The VpnHood Server IP may identify the hosting provider and the country where the VpnHood Server has been set up, but this doesn't provide information about who's running the server or accessing it.  

## Client IP Address

You can configure your VpnHood Server to send your client IP address to the Manager or not. If you decide to send it, VpnHood Manager can perform certain operations to counter DDoS attacks, scanning, and similar hacking attempts. We do not save or log your client IP addresses in the VpnHood Manager database by default unless you explicitly enable it. If you allow your users to keep their IP addresses, you are responsible for your client's privacy policy.  

## Server SSH Credential

We do not save your VpnHood Server credentials unless you explicitly ask us to keep it for future use.  

## Our VpnHood Servers

You can order a VpnHood Server from us so we manage your servers. You can configure how to log your client activity, and you are responsible for ensuring your client's privacy policy is in place.  

## Client Activity

We strive to maintain the privacy of you and your clients as much as possible. You can also have some control over your client's privacy. Depending on the hosting you choose, you may need to track your client's activity to resolve notice(s) of copyright infringement under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).  

We have made every effort to minimize your client's tracking and offer various options. You may not like it, but unfortunately, depending on the law of the country where your VpnHood Server is hosted, it may be mandatory to save some tracks to resolve infringement notices.  

The access server cannot track your user's activities, but VpnHood Servers can do so, just like any other VPN service. VpnHood Servers delete their log files after 30 days by default, but you can change it on your VpnHood Server.  

You can choose one of the following options for your clients' Activities, and you are responsible for your client's privacy policy.  

- **No tracking**  
  You can configure your server not to track anything about user activities and disable or redact all users' IP activity.  

- **Local Port Tracking**  
  You can configure your VpnHood Server just to track the local port opened by your client. In that case, you can trace the user using a device ID or user account for Notices of Claimed Infringement; however, the VpnHood Servers do not store any information about client IPs.  

- **Destination IP Tracking**  
  You can configure your VpnHood Server to log destination endpoints (IP+Port) associated with the client ID.  

- **Full IP tracking**  
  You can configure your VpnHood Server to log destination endpoints (IP and port), as well as your client endpoints associated with the client ID.
